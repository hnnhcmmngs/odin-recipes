Tuscan Ravioli

Description

This is a quick and simple ravioli recipe! It is perfect for anyone who enjoys pasta, garlic, cheese, and tomatoes.

The recipe comes together quickly and easily with many readily available store-bought ingredients. Even though it doesn't take long to make, this dish is wonderful! The spinach provides a freshness to the dish while the sun-dried tomatoes pack the dish with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained
  • 20 ounces refrigerated ravioli

Steps

  1. Add the heavy cream, chicken broth, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and parmesan cheese to a medium saucepan. Whisk over medium-high heat until it starts to thicken.
  2. Add the spinach and sun dried tomatoes and let it simmer until the spinach starts to wilt.
  3. Boil the ravioli according to package instructions.
  4. Serve the sauce over the cooked ravioli and enjoy!
