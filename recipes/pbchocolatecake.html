Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Frosting

Description

This is the ultimate cake for lovers of peanut butter and chocolate!

I make this recipe for my family every year at Christmas. The cake is incredibly rich and delicious, but it is not super difficult to make. I like adding chocolate shavings to the top of it.

Ingredients

Chocolate Cake

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 3 (1 oz) squares semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups whole buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Peanut Butter Frosting

  • 1 1/4 cups butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 5 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 2 (10-inch) round cake pans with nonstick baking spray with flour.
  2. In a small bowl, combine hot water and chocolate. Let stand for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chocolate is mealted and smooth.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soad, baking powder, and salt. Add buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla; beat at medium-low speed with an electric mixer until smooth. With mixer running, add melted chocolate mixture, beating until combined. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans, and bake for 23 to 27 minutes, or until a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes. Remove from pans, and cool completely on wire racks.
  4. Spread Peanut Butter Frosting evenly in between layers and on top and sides of cake. Garnish with chocolate curls, if desired.
